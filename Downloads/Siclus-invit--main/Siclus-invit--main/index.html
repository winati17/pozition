Visioning a City of the Future: Pioneering Innovations to Create a Future-Ready Metropolis


September 22, 2024

at

Baruga Andi Pangerang

is the grand finale of the SICLUS competition, presenting a special event featuring prominent national and international figures. In this gathering, they will collaboratively explore and formulate a shared vision under the theme "Visioning the Sustainable City of the Future." The event will also be enlivened by captivating cultural dance performances and a spectacular concert, designed to entertain and inspire the audience.

What is SICLUS?


Siclus is an annual event organized by SRE Unhas since 2023. Unlike the previous year, this year's Siclus is open to international participants who are also interested in competing.
